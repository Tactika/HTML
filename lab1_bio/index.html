Introduction

Uncle Bob (Robert C. Martin) was a software professional I was introduced to indirectly while browsing YouTube looking for better ways to make my code more readable. His general approach to teaching programmers and project managers how to better understand what 'clean code' is and how it benefits the entire tech community drew me in.

Uncle Bob is a programmer, author, public speaker, educator, and CEO.
